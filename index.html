About

Hi,

My name is Lee Glicklich and I am an undergraduate student at Tufts University studying Economics and Communications & Mass Media Studies.


EXPERIENCE: 

Hyfn New York, NY (June 2015 – August 2015) Summer Intern

Hyfn is an advertising agency headquarted in Hermosa Beach, CA specializing in digital solutions through the integration of creativity, strategy, and technology. I am currently an intern at their New York City office where I work directly with the Managing and Account Directors on research for pitches and current clients, helping construct creative assets, and various projects such as competitive analyses, copywriting, and social audits. I have had the opportunity to work with brands like MILLY, Vonage, and 5 Hour Energy.

 

Tufts Entertainment Board Medford, MA (2013 – Present) Advertising and Social Media Assistant; Event Photographer

The TU Entertainment Board is a student organization responsible for school-wide entertainment events including comedy shows, festivals, topical lectures, and sporting trips. In the past we have had the honor to host Jeffrey Tambor, Reta, and Kenan Thompson. Additionally, I identify opportunities to advertise upcoming events through both traditional and digital media. 

 

Sigma Phi Epsilon Fraternity Medford, MA (2014 – Present) Brother; Communications Committee

As a proud member of SigEp, I not only have a great group of friends, but am offered plenty of philanthropy opportunities to attend each month. Internally, I work with communications committee to raise campus, community and alumnae relations, as well as aid in the development of a new website

 

Tufts sQ! Co-ed Acapella Group (2014 – Present) Treasurer

I am a trained vocalist and vocal percussionist for Tufts sQ!, an auditioned co-ed acapella group on campus. We partake in on-campus and off-campus performances and competitions. As treasurer, I am responsible for all financial oversight, planning, and funding. Furthermore, I assist with composing musical arrangements and advertising upcoming events.

 

Friends of Fort Point Channel (Spring Semester 2015) Spring Intern

Friends of Fort Point is a nonprofit organization to help promote and establish exciting events in the Fort Point district of Boston. I worked directly with the Executive Director as a member of the marketing team to help improve Fort Point’s social media presence and usage

 

The Ridgefield Playhouse (Summer 2014) Summer Intern
